Neighborhood recap

Summer Solstice, rain or shine.

Thunderstorms changed our plans, but not the evening. We moved the party inside the clubhouse, shared a meal, and enjoyed time with the neighbors who stopped by. It was a smaller gathering than planned, but a warm reminder that community doesn’t require perfect weather—or a huge crowd.

Campbell Drive speed humps

Help us move the petition forward.

Speeding and cut-through traffic on Campbell Drive remain neighborhood concerns. Neighboring subdivisions have had speed humps installed, and residents have seen how much they can help slow traffic on through streets.

Only residents or property owners inside the city-defined petition area may sign, and only one signature is accepted per household. If you support the project and may be eligible, contact Robyn Dunlap at 816-835-6739.
